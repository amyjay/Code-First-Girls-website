Welcome to the Durham Café Collection!

Hello, It’s nice to have you here!


We are a hopefully fair and unbiased independent group, that have taken the time to test and trial the coffee shops of Durham so that you don't have to. It has been a real hardship slogging away, drinking tea after tea, eating cake after cake, in the search of some friendly, cosy, yummy and ethical coffee shops that provide both great food and a better atmosphere.


We are but humble students, and in no way claim to be experts, however we hope our thoughts can be helpful to you in finding a refuge from the harsh Durham wind and rain.


So thanks for stopping by!
