Welcome to the Durham Café Collection!

It’s nice to have you here!


We are an independent group that have taken the time to test and trial the coffee shops of Durham to give you our verdict on the best places to sip a cup of tea or nibble on some cake. Throughout our research we have tried to be as fair and as unbiased as possible, so we hope you find that our reviews answer your questions and spur your passion to find the best café to meet your preferences. That said, we are driven by a desire to find friendly, cosy, yummy and ethical coffee shops that not only provide us with great food and drink, but also provide a great atmosphere to customers and local initiatives.


We don’t claim to be experts, but we hope that our thoughts can offer you some help in finding a refuge from the harsh Durham wind and rain to shelter for a well-deserved break, meet up with friends for a natter, or make your work-load feel a little lighter.


Thanks for stopping by!
