Jumping Bean Cafe


We love ethical living, and we love drinking tea (and coffee... and eating cake...). We wanted to combine the passions and find the best places to drink ethical drinks and eat ethical cakes. Enter Jumping Bean Cafe- located just off North Road on Neville Street, this vegetarian caf√© provides great-value cakes, sandwiches, tapas and more. They use fairly traded and free-range products, sourcing their vegetables from Robinsons across the road, and cook all their food on site with minimal waste. They also support initiatives like Foodcycle and Ideas Hive, and host a variety of different events, so we would thoroughly recommend Jumping Bean as a tasty and ethical option!
