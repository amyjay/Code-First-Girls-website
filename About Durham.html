About Us

Welcome to Durham, the quaint and quirky heart of the North East of England.


Known for its cobbled bailey, busy market place and towering cathedral, Durham is also not short of places to enjoy a cup of tea. This is why we’ve created The Durham Café Collection: to give you our top tips on where to nibble on cakes and sip the best tea that the North East can offer.


We are passionate about ensuring that you not only find shelter from the cold streets and sporadic downpours, but that you can also leave feeling happy that your choice of café has contributed to the wider community. When carrying out our reviews, we made sure to consider a large number of factors such as what the ambience is like, but also whether they offer Fairtrade coffee, whether they use local suppliers for their food and milk, or whether they are part of a larger chain which is renowned for tax evasion or other forms of unfriendly behaviour. We have tried to be fair about all of the cafés that we review but we are personally particularly keen on the adorable independent cafés that are unique to Durham, so you won’t find them anywhere else.


Our dream is that you will be inspired to try somewhere new that fills your tummy and offers you sanctuary from the business of life… or at least allows you to get on with your business in a friendly and comfy setting!


Happy tea-drinking,
Amy and Eve
